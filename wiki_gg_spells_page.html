Spells

From Eldenpedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Spells come in two types: sorceries and incantations.

Note, for the on-weapon enchantments to work such as Poison Armament, the weapon you intend to enchant needs to be active in the right hand and the spellcasting implement active in the left, and then the enchantment can be cast.
