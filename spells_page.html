                      Magic Spells | Elden Ring Wiki


                      Magic Spells in Elden Ring are cast by players in the form of Sorceries and Incantations. From conjuring glintstone pebbles to invoking the power of a dragon's breath, spells cover a wide variety of offensive, defensive, and utility functions. This page covers the basics of using spells and each spell available in the game.

                       

                      Shadow of the Erdtree New Incantations & Sorceries

                      With the release of Shadow of the Erdtree, 42 new powerful Magic Spells are added to the already stacked 171 Magic Spells in the base game. This leads to a total of 213 Magic Spells available between the base game and the new content added with the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC.

                      Of the newly added 42 Spells, 14 of them are Sorceries and the remaining 28 are Incantations.

                      This page is up to date with all this new content. Players will be able to search and find all Shadow of the Erdtree New Magic Spells here, as well as to learn about their Use, Requirements, Scaling, ideal Builds, and much more regarding the new Sorceries and Incantations added with the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC! This comprehensive guide will let players optimize their Spellcasting abilities and help them reach new heights with their Sorceries and Incantations.

                       

                      Spellcasting Restrictions 

                      • Spellcasting requires a catalyst:
                        • Sorceries: Staves, Carian Sorcery Sword
                        • Incantations: Sacred Seals, Staff of the Great Beyond
                      • Casting Sorceries and Incantations consumes FP and Stamina.
                      • You can restore FP by finding and killing Cerulean (blue) Teardrop Scarabs or complete groups of enemies.

                      Memory Slots

                      Memory Slots determine the number of spells you can memorize.

                      You can increase the number of available memory slots using the following:

                      • Memory Stones
                      • Moon of Nokstella

                      Casting Time

                      Different spells will take different lengths of time to cast, typically with more powerful spells taking longer to cast. Casting Time can be influenced by a variety of factors, including:

                      • Dexterity
                      • Equipment
                        • Radagon Icon
                        • Azur's Glintstone Staff

                      Additionally, some slower spells can be quick-cast if used as part of a combo; e.g. any spell or regular melee → Carian Slicer, or Catch Flame → Magma Breath will result in much faster cast times for Carian Slicer and Magma Breath.
                      Dragon Communion Incantations and Scarlet Aeonia are not affected by different casting speed.

                      All Legendary Magic Spells in Elden Ring

                      To unlock the Legendary Sorceries and Incantations Trophy and Achievement you will need to acquire the following spells:

                      1. Flame of the Fell God
                      2. Founding Rain of Stars
                      3. Elden Stars
                      4. Ranni's Dark Moon
                      5. Comet Azur
                      6. Stars of Ruin
                      7. Greyoll's Roar
